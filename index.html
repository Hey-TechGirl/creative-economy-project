Creative Economy Project.

A Master's Thesis research that attempts to investigate how creative workers have been dealing with digital technologies.

About the Project.

The main objective of this research is to investigate the effects of digitalization on SMEs and freelancer workers of creative industries. It is worth noting that in this case, the definition of digitalization used in this study goes beyond the transformation of data from the physical material into bits, such as reducing the use of paper when using applications for storing information such as spreadsheets and databases.

The digitization definition used here is the ability to connect and interact information in the generation of solutions, management, organization, and creation of value through the exploitation of digital data. Moreover, when it comes to creative industry workers, the ability to increase the creativity employed in their products and services.

Understanding the complexity and variety of industries in which this workforce is distributed, this research aims to try to clarify similarity and divergences in the difficulties and advantages faced by this group of people when interacting with digitization in their work tasks.

How to participate?

Industries

You need to work in one of the these industries: Literature, Music, Performing Arts, Visual Arts, Film and Video, Crafts, Design, Advertising, Architecture, Publishing, Fashion, Software, Television and Radio, Video and computer game and Internet.

Experience

You need to have worked as a freelancer or been working in a small company with up to fifty employees. This study looks for creative workers who have experience outside large corporations and understand the labor market's difficulty for freelancers and SMEs.

Worldwide

It does not matter what country you live in or where you originally from. This study extends to any location in the globe, regardless your gender, age and any other variant. It is important that the participants have different perspectives.

Your opinion is important!

Only you can tell your experiences, your difficulties and your overcomes.

We need to hear from you to include your perspective in the statistics.
